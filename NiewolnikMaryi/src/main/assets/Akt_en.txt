<b> THE ACT OF SACRIFICATION TO JESUS CHRIST BY MARY'S HANDS </b>
<br/> <br/>
By. St. Ludwika Maria Grignon de Montfort, "A treatise on perfect devotion to the Blessed Virgin Mary" <br> <br/>
Oh, eternal, incarnate of Wisdom! Oh, beloved and reverent most beloved Jesus, true God and true man, only begotten Son of the Eternal Father and Mary, always Virgin! I give You the deepest reverence in the womb and glory of Your Father in eternity and in the virgin womb of Mary, Your most worthy Mother, during Your incarnation. <br> <br/>
Thank you, you have destroyed yourself, taking the form of a servant to save me from Satan's cruel slavery. I praise You and love that You surrendered to Mary, Your Holy Mother, to make me a faithful slave through her. But unfortunately! In my ingratitude and unfaithfulness, I did not keep the promises I solemnly made to You at the baptism of Saint. I have not fulfilled my obligations; I am not worthy to be called a child or Your slave, and because there is nothing in me that does not deserve Your anger, I dare not approach Your Holy and Most Majesty myself. Therefore, I am resorting to the intercession and mercy of Your Blessed Mother, whom You gave me as an Mediator with me. Through her mighty mediation, I hope to obtain repentance and forgiveness from you, as well as true wisdom and perseverance in her. <br> <br/>
I greet you, Immaculate Virgin, living shrine of the Deity, in which the eternal, hidden wisdom wants to receive the worship and worship of angels and people. I greet You, Queen of heaven and earth, whose rule except God is surrendered. I greet You, Refuge of sinners whose mercy has not disappointed anyone; hear my plea, full of fervent desire for God's wisdom, and accept the vows and sacrifices I make to you humbly. <br> <br/>
<br/> <br/>
I, ... an unfaithful sinner, renew and approve today in the face of your baptism vows. I renounce Satan and his pride and his works forever, and I devote myself entirely to Jesus Christ, the Incarnate Wisdom, to follow him, carrying my cross all the days of my life. Let me be more faithful to Him than ever before, today I choose you, Mary, in the face of the whole heavenly court, for my Mother and Lady. I entrust to You and as a slave Your body and my soul, internal and external goods, even the value of my good deeds, both past and present and future, leaving you with the total and complete right to dispose of me and all without exception, as to me, as you please, for the greater glory of God, in time and eternity. <br> <br/>
Receive, gracious Virgin, this small sacrifice of my slavery to honor and imitate the submission which eternal wisdom has shown to Your motherhood; to honor the authority you both have over me, a small worm and a wretched sinner; as a thank you for the privileges that the Holy Trinity has kindly given you. I assure you that from now on, as a true slave, I want to seek Your glory and obey You in everything. Wonderful Mother, introduce me to Your dear Son as an eternal slave, that He would redeem me through You, so He will accept me through You. Mother of mercy, give me the grace of God's true wisdom and accept me among those whom you love, teach, lead, feed and defend like your children and servants. Virgin faithful, let me always and in everything be such a perfect disciple, follower and slave of the Incarnate Wisdom, Jesus Christ, Your Son, that through Your mediation and through Your example I may come to the fullness of His age on earth and His glory in heaven. Amen.
