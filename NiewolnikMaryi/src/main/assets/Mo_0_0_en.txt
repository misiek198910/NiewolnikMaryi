<h3>Magnificat - Thanksgiving song of the Blessed Virgin Mary</h3>
<br/>
My soul praises the Lord, and my spirit rejoices in God my Savior. For he looked at the shortness of his handmaid;
For, from now on, all nations will call me blessed. For he has done great things for me that is powerful, and his holy name.
And his mercy from generation to generation that fear him.
He made strength with his arm, scattered the delicious in their minds. He knocked down the mighty from the capital and raised the humble.
He filled the hungry with goods, and let the rich go with nothing.
He received Israel his servant, remembering his mercy.
As he spoke to our fathers, Abraham and his descendants forever.
