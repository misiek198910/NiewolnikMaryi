<h3> Veni, Creator Spiritus </h3>
<br/>
1. O Creator, the Spirit, come down, lead the hearts of faithful to You circle, send Heavenly grace to your breasts, which are the work of your hands. <br> </br>
2. You are called the Comforter, You are the gift of Almighty God, You are the anointing of our souls, the living spring, the love, the ardor of fire. <br> </br>
3. You have grace sevenfold, for you have strength from the right hand of the Father, promised us by the Father, you enrich our language with speech. <br> </br>
4. Illuminate our thoughts with light, pour holy love into our hearts, And confirm the weak weakness of our bodies with the constancy of your strength. <br> </br>
5. Drive the enemy out, and give peace for everlasting time. Under your steadfast leadership, let us avoid all harm. <br> </br>
6. Let us know the Father through You, let the Son also be known, And let us, the Spirit of both of them, believe in You with all our might. <br> </br>
7. Be to God the Father, glory, honor, the Son who raised from the dead, And to him who comforts us, to eternity whose end is a stranger.
Amen.
