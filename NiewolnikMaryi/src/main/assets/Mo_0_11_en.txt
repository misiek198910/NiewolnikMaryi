<h3>Ave Maris Stella – New Version</h3>
<br/>
1. Hail, Star of the Sea,<br/>
Great Mother of God,<br/>
Virgin ever holy,<br/>
Heaven’s blessed gate.<br/>
<br/>
2. You, who Gabriel<br/>
Greeted with his word,<br/>
Confirm us in peace,<br/>
Undo Eve’s ancient name.<br/>
<br/>
3. Free the guilty from chains,<br/>
Give the blind their sight;<br/>
Drive our miseries away,<br/>
Obtain for us all grace.<br/>
<br/>
4. Show yourself a Mother:<br/>
By your fervent prayers<br/>
Move Him who, being your Son,<br/>
Chose to dwell on earth.<br/>
<br/>
5. O Virgin most renowned,<br/>
Model of humility,<br/>
Freed from sin, may we<br/>
Live in meekness constantly.<br/>
<br/>
6. Grant us a life made pure,<br/>
Make our journey sure;<br/>
Let us see Christ's face,<br/>
Find in Him eternal grace.<br/>
<br/>
7. Glory be to God the Father,<br/>
Praise to Christ the Son,<br/>
Equal honor ever be<br/>
To the Spirit, Three-in-One. Amen.<br/>
