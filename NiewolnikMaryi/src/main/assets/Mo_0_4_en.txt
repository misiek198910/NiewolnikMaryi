<h3>O Jesus living in Mary! </h3>
<b>St. Ludwik Grignon de Montfort - translated by Magdalena Tuszyńska, from the text in English </b>
<br/>
<br/>
O Jesus living in Mary,
Come and live in your servants,
In the spirit of your holiness,
In fulfilling your power,
In the truth of your virtues,
In the perfection of your roads,
In the unity of your secrets,
Relieve all enemy strength
In Your spirit, for the Father's glory. Amen.
