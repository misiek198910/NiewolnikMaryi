<h3> Litany of the Holy Spirit - (private use only) </h3>
<br/>
<b> Kyrie, elejson </b>. Christ, Eleisons. Kyrie, Eleison. <br> </br>
<b> Christ, hear us. </b> Christ, hear us. <br> </br>
<b> Father of heaven </b> God, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Son, Redeemer of the world, God </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit, God </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Trinity, one God </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit of wisdom </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit of understanding </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit of counsel </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit of bravery </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit of skill </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit of godliness </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit of God fear </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit, light of the prophets and apostles </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit, our Comforter </b>, have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Be merciful to us </b> spare us, Holy Spirit of God. <br> </br>
<b> From audacious trust </b> save us, Holy Spirit of God. <br> </br>
<b> From resisting recognized Christian truth, save us, Holy Spirit of God. <br> </br>
<b> From unkindness and envy to the brothers of God's grace, save us, Holy Spirit of God. <br> </br>
<b> From hardness of heart </b> save us, Holy Spirit of God. <br> </br>
<b> From neglecting repentance to death </b> save us, Holy Spirit of God. <br> </br>
<b> From eternal damnation </b> save us, Holy Spirit of God. <br> </br>
<b> Through the wonderful work of the conception of Jesus Christ </b> save us, Holy Spirit of God. <br> </br>
<b> By coming in tongues of fire </b> save us, Holy Spirit of God. <br> </br>
<b> That You would deign to rule and keep Your Holy Church </b>, we sinners, we ask You, hear us, Holy Spirit. <br> </br>
<b> That You would deign to strengthen us in piety and humility </b>, we sinners, we ask You, hear us, Holy Spirit. <br> </br>
<b> That You would deign to grant us constancy and fortitude </b>, we sinners ask You, hear us, Holy Spirit. <br> </br>
<b> That you would deign to elevate our thoughts to the desire of heavenly things, we sinners, we ask You, hear us, Holy Spirit. <br> </br>
<b> That You deign to purify us and sacrifice us for a decent dwelling, </b> we sinners are asking You, hear us, Holy Spirit. <br> </br>
<b> That You deign to comfort us in our sufferings </b>, we sinners, we ask You, hear us, Holy Spirit. <br> </br>
<b> That you would deign to lead us all to the eternal glory </b>, we sinners, we ask you, hear us, Holy Spirit. <br> </br>
<b> That we may love You and avoid sins for You </b>, we sinners ask You, hear us, Holy Spirit. <br> </br>
<br/>
<br/>
<b> Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world </b>, spare us, Lord.
<b> Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world </b>, hear us, Lord.
<b> Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world </b>, have mercy on us.
<br/>
<br/>
W. A pure heart, God create in me. <br/>
A. And renew a righteous spirit in my belly. W. Lord, hear our prayers. <br/>
A. Let our cry come to you. <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Let us pray: Come, Holy Spirit, deign to fill the hearts of Your faithful and kindle the fire of Your love in them, which You have gathered all nations in unity of faith, that Your Most Holy Grace may enlighten our hearts and save us from all evil. Amen.
