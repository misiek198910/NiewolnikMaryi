<h3> Litany of the Most Holy Name of Jesus </h3>
<br/>
<b> Kyrie, Eleison. </b> Christ, Eleison. Kyrie, Eleison. <br> </br>
<b> Christ, hear us. </b> Christ, hear us. <br> </br>
<b> God the Father of Heaven </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Son, Redeemer of the world, God, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit, God </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Trinity, one God, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, Son of the living God, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, Fatherly brightness, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, radiance of eternal light, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, King of Glory, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, sun of justice, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, Son of the Virgin Mary, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Beloved Jesus, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, most admirable, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, mighty God, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, father forever, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, great guide of souls, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, most powerful, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, the most patient, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, most obedient, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, meek and humble of heart, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, lover of cleanliness, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus our lover </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, God of peace, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, giver of life, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, example of virtues, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, thirsting for our souls, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus our God </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, our escape, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, father of the poor, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, treasure of the faithful, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, good shepherd, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, true light, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, Eternal Wisdom, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, infinite goodness, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, our dear and our life, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, joy of Angels, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, King of the Patriarchs, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, Master of the Apostles, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, Teacher of the Evangelists, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, bravery of Martyrs, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, light of believers, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, purity of virgins, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Jesus, crown of all Saints, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<br/>
<br/>
<b> Be merciful to us, </b> spare us, Jesus. <b> Be merciful to us, </b> hear us, Jesus.
<b> From all evil, </b> deliver us, Jesus. <b> From everyone's sin, save us, Jesus. <b> From Your wrath </b> deliver us, Jesus. <b> From satanic snares </b> save us, Jesus. <b> From the spirit of impurity, </b> deliver us, Jesus. <b> From eternal damnation </b> save us, Jesus. <b> From the neglect of Your inspirations, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through the mystery of your holy incarnation, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through your birth, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through your childhood, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through your most holy life </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through your labors, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> By dying in the Garden of Gethsemane and Your Passion, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through your cross and your abandonment, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> By your fainting, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through your death and burial, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> By your resurrection, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> By your ascension, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through the Blessed Sacrament, your institution, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through Your joys, </b> save us, Jesus. <b> Through Your glory, </b> save us, Jesus.
<br/>
<br/>
Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world, spare us, Lord. Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world, hear us, Lord. Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world, have mercy on us.
<br/>
<br/>
W. Jesus, hear us. <br/>
O Jesus, hear us. <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Let us pray: Lord Jesus Christ, who said: Ask and you will receive, seek and you will find, knock and it will be opened to you: we beg You, let us be overwhelmed with the highest love for You, that we may love You with our whole heart, words and deeds and never they never stopped adoring you. Grant, O Lord, that we may be constantly overwhelmed by the profound love of Your Holy Name, for You never refuse Your protection to those whom You confirm in Your love for You. Who live and reign for ever and ever. Amen.
