<h3> Litany of the Sacred Heart of Jesus </h3>
<br/>
<b> Kyrie, Eleison. </b> Christ, Eleison. Kyrie, Eleison. <br> </br>
<b> Christ, hear us. </b> Christ, hear us. <br> </br>
<b> God the Father of Heaven </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Son, Redeemer of the world, God, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Spirit, God </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Holy Trinity, one God, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, Son of the Eternal Father, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, formed by the Holy Spirit in the Virgin Mother's womb, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, most closely united to the Word of God, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, infinite in majesty, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, temple of God, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, Tabernacle of the Most High, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, house of God and heavenly gate, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, burning fire of love, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, Treasury of justice and love, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, full of goodness and love, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, bottomless depths of all virtues, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, most worthy of all praise, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, King and unity of all hearts, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, in whom are all treasures of wisdom and knowledge, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, in whom all the fullness of God dwells, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, in whom the Father is very pleased, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, from whose fullness we have received all, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, the eternal longing for the world, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, patient and great mercy, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, generous to all who call on You, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, source of life and holiness </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, propitiation for our sins, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, overflowing with loathing, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, bruised for our iniquities, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, obedient unto death, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, pierced by a spear, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, source of all consolation, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, our life and resurrection, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, our peace and reconciliation, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, bloody offering for sins, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, salvation of those who trust in You, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, hope of those who die in You, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<b> Heart of Jesus, delight of all Saints, </b> have mercy on us. <br> </br>
<br/>
<br/>
Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world, spare us, Lord. Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world, hear us, Lord. Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world, have mercy on us.
<br/>
<br/>
W. Jesus, meek and humble of heart.
A. Make our hearts like yours.
<br/>
<br/>
Let us pray: Almighty, eternal God, look upon the Heart of Your beloved Son and on the satisfaction and glory that He gives You sinners from us; Let us, who beg Your mercy, beg Your mercy, and grant forgiveness in the name of Your Son Jesus Christ, who lives with You and reigns for ever and ever. Amen.
