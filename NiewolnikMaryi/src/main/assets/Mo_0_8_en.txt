<b> Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world, <b /> spare us, Lord. <b> Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world, <b /> hear us, Lord. <b> Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world, <b /> have mercy on us.

<br/>
W. Pray for us, Holy Mother of God. <br/>
A. That we may become worthy of the promises of Christ.
<br/>
<br/>
Let us pray: We ask You, Lord God, allow us, Your servants, to enjoy the lasting health of soul and body, and through the intercession of Mary, always Virgin, deign to free us from worldly troubles and grant us eternal joy. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.
