I, ... an unfaithful sinner, renew and approve today in the face of your baptism vows. I renounce Satan and his pride and his works forever, and I devote myself entirely to Jesus Christ, the Incarnate Wisdom, to follow him, carrying my cross all the days of my life. Let me be more faithful to Him than ever before, today I choose you, Mary, in the face of the whole heavenly court, for my Mother and Lady. I entrust to You and as a slave Your body and my soul, internal and external goods, even the value of my good deeds, both past and present and future, leaving you with the total and complete right to dispose of me and all without exception, as to me, as you please, for the greater glory of God, in time and eternity. <br> <br/>
Receive, gracious Virgin, this small sacrifice of my slavery to honor and imitate the submission which eternal wisdom has shown to Your motherhood; to honor the authority you both have over me, a small worm and a wretched sinner; as a thank you for the privileges that the Holy Trinity has kindly given you. I assure you that from now on, as a true slave, I want to seek Your glory and obey You in everything. Wonderful Mother, introduce me to Your dear Son as an eternal slave, that He would redeem me through You, so He will accept me through You. Mother of mercy, give me the grace of God's true wisdom and accept me among those whom you love, teach, lead, feed and defend like your children and servants. Virgin faithful, let me always and in everything be such a perfect disciple, follower and slave of the Incarnate Wisdom, Jesus Christ, Your Son, that through Your mediation and through Your example I may come to the fullness of His age on earth and His glory in heaven. Amen.
