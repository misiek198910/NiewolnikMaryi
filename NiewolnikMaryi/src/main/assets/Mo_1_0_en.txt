<h1> Service for the first Saturday of the month </h1>
<br/>
Devotion to the Immaculate Heart of Mary is a special gift of God for our times. During the apparitions of Fatima after the terrifying vision of hell, the children of Fatima heard from Mary's lips: "You have seen hell where the souls of poor sinners go. To save them, God wants to spread the devotion to my Immaculate Heart in the world.
If what I tell you is done, many will be saved from hell and there will be peace in the world. "A few years later, on December 10, 1925, she appeared to Sister Lucia (whom God left on earth as the only one of the three little shepherds) with the Child in her arms and a Heart surrounded by thorns. The Child said:
Have compassion with the Heart of Your Most Holy Mother, surrounded by thorns with which ungrateful people hurt them again and again, and there is no one who would draw them out by the act of rewarding them.
<br/>
<br/>
Through Lucia, Mary asked all of us:
<br/>
<br/>
My daughter, see, My Heart is surrounded by thorns with which ungrateful people constantly hurt through blasphemy and infidelity. At least you, try to bring me joy and make it known to me
name that I will come at the hour of death with the favors needed for salvation to all those who will confess for five months on the first Saturdays, receive Holy Communion
, they will pray the rosary and for fifteen minutes of meditating on the fifteen mysteries of the rosary they will accompany me with the intention of making amends.
<br/>
<br/>
<h1> Atonement to the Immaculate Heart of Mary </h1>
<br/>
On the night of May 29-30, 1930, Lucia heard an explanation from Jesus about the first five Saturdays:
Daughter, the motive is simple: there are five kinds of insults and blasphemies uttered against the Immaculate Heart of Mary.
<br/>
<br/>
<br> 1. Blasphemy against the Immaculate Conception. </br>
<br> 2. Against her virginity. </br>
<br> 3. Against God's Motherhood, while at the same time she is recognized only as the Mother of man. </br>
<br> 4. Blasphemy of those who openly instill in the hearts of children indifference, contempt and even hatred of this Immaculate Mother. </br>
<br> 5. The atrocities of those who claim her directly in her holy images. This, dear daughter, is the motive that made the Immaculate Heart of Mary ask Me for this little act of reward. And besides for her, I wanted to touch
my mercy, that she would forgive those souls who had the misfortune to offend her. As for you, strive constantly with your prayers and sacrifices to move Me to show these poor souls mercy.
<br/>
<br/>
Jesus said to Sister Lucy: "It is true, my daughter, that many souls begin, but few finish, and those who finish have the goal of receiving the promised favors. But I prefer those who send
first Saturdays in order to reward the Immaculate Heart of your Heavenly Mother, than those who will send away fifteen, heartlessly and with indifference ". </br>
Our Lady indicated the essence of the service: remuneration, not receiving specific gifts that we are asking for. However, she - full of infinite goodness - repays all those who practice
it is a service, a deepened bond with her. Could there be anything greater? This devotion creates a special bond of love between the person celebrating it and Mary. She promises to come to their rescue in an hour
departing from this world, ensuring eternal salvation.
<br/>
<br/>
<h1> How do I practice worship on the first Saturday of the month? </h1>
<br/>
1. Proceed to the sacrament of penance and reconciliation with the intention of rewarding the Immaculate Heart of Mary. This intention can be aroused before confession or while we receive absolution.
A slightly earlier confession will also be important, provided that we wish to satisfy the Immaculate Heart of Mary and remain in a state of grace. Before confession, you can pray with the following words:
<b> The Most Holy Virgin Mary, offers you this confession as reparation for the insults (<i> or name the one chosen from five of the insults </i>) that your Immaculate Heart experiences from us.
Accept, O good Mother, this reward as an act of my love, honor and devotion. Be blessed, full of grace through whom salvation has come into the world. <b> <br> </br>
2. Receive Holy Communion and offer it as a reward to the Immaculate Heart of Mary. After receiving the body of Christ, you can pray with the words: <br> </br>
<b> Most Holy Virgin Mary, I offer you this Holy Communion as a reward for the insults (<i> or to name a chosen one of the five insults </i>) experienced by your Immaculate Heart.
Deign to accept my atonement for our sins with which you are so badly wounded by us. I love you and I am all yours. <br> </br>
3. Pray any part of the Rosary (<i> five mysteries </i>). After each of the Mysteries, the prayer conveyed in the Fatima message is said: <b> O my Jesus, forgive us our sins, save us from the fire of hell,
lead all souls to heaven and especially help those who need Your mercy the most. </b> <br> </br>
4. For fifteen minutes, ponder the chosen mystery or all the mysteries of the recited part of the rosary. The word of God will help in this meditation. you can choose a longer fragment of the Gospel or one sentence, or even a few words.
meditate on them, repeat them, meditate on them. One can contemplate one image from a given mystery, "with the eyes of the heart" gazing at Christ, remaining with Him and His Mother. <br> </br>
