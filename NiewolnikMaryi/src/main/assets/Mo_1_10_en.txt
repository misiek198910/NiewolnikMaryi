<h1> Blessed <h1 />
<br/>
Blessed by the wonderful splendor of virginity. Her name shines forever! Blessed because through her the family of Adam was renewed, and expelled from the Father's house, they rose again. Blessed, because the Great One has dwelt in her little womb, she fills the heavens, and they cannot embrace Him! Blessed because she gave birth to the Ancient of Days who gave birth to Adam, through her all creatures were renewed. Blessed because she gave milk to him, at whose command the sea waves stopped. Blessed because the Lord of ages, who carries the world with its hidden strength, she carried, embraced and held her as her Child. Blessed because of her was born a Savior, who bound the dragon and gave peace to the earth. Blessed, for she could lay her pure lips on the lips of Him, from whose heat the face of the fiery Seraphim must hide. Blessed because with her pure milk she nourished the one from whom the worlds suck life as if from a mother's breast! Blessed because her Son blesses all the blessed. Praised be He who was born in a holy way of her purity!
