<h1> The Sorrowful Queen of Poland <h1 />
<br/>
Mary, Mother of the Polish land, our hope, Sorrowful Queen of Poland. In our rosary prayer today, we stand before you. We want to remain with Your Son in the hours of His agony, we want to look at His battered face, we want to take up our cross in our daily work, our toil, our problems, and follow Christ's path to Calvary. Mary, support us who are tormented by wounds and physical pains. We are so often faced with anxiety, dilemma and breakdown, keep your spirits up, Mary. To us who are humiliated, often deprived of human rights and dignity, grant us courage and perseverance. To us, who make every effort to renew the face of the earth, our Polish land, in the spirit of the Gospel, show your motherly protection, Mary. To us who struggle and toil for Truth, Justice, Love, Peace and Freedom in our homeland, give a helping hand.
