<h1> To Our Lady of the Scapular <h1 />
<br/>
O Blessed Immaculate Virgin, Ornament and Light of Mount Carmel, who with the eye of a more peculiar goodness you look at everyone who is clothed with your holy robe, look at me graciously and cover me with the mantle of your motherly protection, help my weakness with your strength, enlighten with your wisdom the darkness of my soul, increase my faith, hope and love in me. Deign to decorate my soul with such graces and virtues, that it may always be the way to your Divine Son and to you. Never leave me in my life, console me with your most grateful presence and introduce me to the Most Holy Trinity as your son and devoted servant to You, that I may praise and glorify You in heaven forever. Amen.
