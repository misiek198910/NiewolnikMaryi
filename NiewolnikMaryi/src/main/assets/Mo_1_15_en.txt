<h1> Hymn To Mary <h3 />
<br/>
Blessed are You, Mary, for here are you fulfilled the mysteries of symbols given by all the prophets. Praised be the Father, who sent the only-begotten, and he appeared in Mary's womb and freed us from error and exalted her memory in heaven and on earth! Blessed are you who conceived, blessed are you who conceived, blessed are you who fed with the milk of the host himself.
Blessed are you, for from your womb shone light that enveloped the whole world; he also sings your praise. Hello, our escape! Hello, our glory! For through You our family has been lifted up to heaven. Therefore, pray for us, God born by You, that He would send serenity and peace for His Church. By the power of your prayer, may He grant peace to the earth and to all that dwell on it, O Mother of the Most High. Honor to him who came and was born of Mary, and made her mother, and was born of her a son.
