<h1> PRAYER TO OUR LADY OF CZĘSTOCHOWA I <h1 />
<br/>
O most glorious Queen of Heaven and Earth, Holy Mary of Częstochowa! Behold, I, Your unworthy servant, stand before You; I beseechingly raise my hands to You and from the bottom of my heart I cry: O Most Compassionate Mother! Save me, because only in You is my hope; if you don't listen to me, who will I go to? I know, dear Mother, that Your compassionate heart will be moved by my prayers and will hear me in my need, because the Divine Almighty rests in Your hand, whose pleasure lies in You. So, Mary! I am begging you, stand up and use your mighty power, dissipating all my sufferings, pouring a blissful balm into my aching soul. O most noble Comforter of all the afflicted; and therefore my special Guardian. It is true that I feel it in the depths of my soul that I am unworthy of my sins for you to give me mercy, but I am begging you, dear Mother! Do not look at me through these my sins, but look through the merits of Your beloved Son and through His Most Holy Blood, which He shed for me on the shameful cross; Remember, dear Mother, the sufferings that you experienced while standing under the cross and half-died looking at the death of Your dear Son. So by remembering all of this, O Mother! Do not refuse my humble request, but kindly hear it, and being comforted by You, I will adore You with a grateful heart until I die. Amen.
