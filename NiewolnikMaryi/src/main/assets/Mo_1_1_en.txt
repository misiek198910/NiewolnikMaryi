<h1> Chaplet of Mary's Bloody Tears, history </h1>
<br/>
The Chaplet to Mary's Bloody Tears was received on November 8, 1929 by Sister Amalia, co-founder of the Institute of the Missionaries of Jesus Crucified in Brazil.
In one vision, the Lord Jesus conveyed the content of this prayer, saying: "Pray to me through my Mother's tears. Whatever people ask me through my Mother's tears, I will gladly give it to them."
Another time, the Mother of God, holding a rosary of luminous beads in her hand, said to Sister Amalia: "My child, I will tell you about the rosary in my hands. I called it the chaplet of tears. Being here with me, seeing this chaplet in my hands, remember that
that it means mercy, love and pain ... This chaplet of my blessed tears tells you that your mother loves you. Use this gift by praying with trust and love. "
She assured me: "My Son wants to honor me in a special way and he will gladly grant all favors through my tears. This chaplet will be used to convert many sinners, especially those who are possessed by an evil spirit."
Our Lady expressed a wish that families and monasteries would pray with the chaplet of love for her bloody tears every day at 9 p.m. On May 15, 1934, the Holy See approved the service,
The Chaplet of Mary's Bloody Tears has great power to crush the might of our enemy - the devil. On November 21, 1982, Our Lady cried tears of blood in the fig of the Spirit Rose in the Italian town of Erbano. She said, "Pray the Chaplet of Bloody Tears! Spread it! The evil spirit must escape from this place where this chaplet is recited."

<h1> What does it consist of? </h1>
<br/>
The chaplet consists of seven parts.
<br/>
<br/>
- Initial prayer <br/>
- Prayer on large beads <br/>
- On small beads <br/>
- Closing prayer <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
<h1> Initial prayer </h1>
<br/>
O Jesus, nailed to the cross, we fall at your feet and offer you the bloody tears of her who sympathized with you with the greatest love, accompanying you on your painful way of the cross.
Good Master, let us understand with love the meaning of the bloody tears of Your Most Holy Mother, and by fulfilling Your holy will here on earth, make us worthy to adore You and worship You for all eternity in heaven. Amen.
<br/>
<br/>
<h1> On large beads </h1>
<br/>
O Jesus, look at the bloody tears of her who loved You the most already here on earth, and I still love You the most in heaven.
<br/>
<br/>
<h1> On small beads </h1>
<br/>
O Jesus, hear our prayers - through the bloody tears of Your Most Holy Mother (7 times).
<br/>
<br/>
<h1> Closing Prayer </h1>
<br/>
O Jesus, look at the bloody tears of her who loved you most already here on earth, and still loves you the most in heaven (3 times).
<br/>
<br/>
O Mary, Mother of Sorrows, Mother of Mercy and Mother of Mercy, unite our prayers with your petitions, so that your Divine Son, Jesus whom we invoke, will hear our cry, and through your cause
with his mother's bloody tears, he gave us the favors that we implore him for, and led to eternal happiness. Amen.
