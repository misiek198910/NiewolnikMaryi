<h1> PRAYER TO OUR LADY CZĘSTOCHOWA II <h1 />
<br/>
Virgin Mother of God, praise God Mary! Queen of the World and Queen of Poland! Holy Virgin, Mother of God of Częstochowa! Behold, I fall at Your feet, I flee to Your protection. Receive me and cover me with the cloak of your goodness. I feel that I am unworthy of Your considerations, that because of my sins I have withdrawn from You, I am asking You through the most precious Blood that Your Son, Jesus Christ, poured out for me, do not throw me away from you. I renew to you all the good resolutions that I have made in my life. Supported by Your help, I promise that I will endure in them until death. Deign to accept, Holy Virgin, this act of my devotion to You and redeem for me in Your Son, Jesus Christ, a living faith, an unshakable attachment to the Holy Catholic Church, a strong hope, a great, noble and effective love. O Mary of Częstochowa, who has been famous for over six hundred years in this case, I am asking you humbly so that by worshiping your miraculous image I would deserve to be seen in the land of heaven, gracious and merciful Lady. Always be my Mother, O Mary of Częstochowa, as I wish to be your child. Amen. Mary, Queen of Poland! Mary, Queen of Poland! I am with you, I remember! I am with you, I remember! I am watching! (3x)
