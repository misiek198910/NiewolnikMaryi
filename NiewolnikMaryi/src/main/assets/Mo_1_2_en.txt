<h1> ACT OF COMPLETELY GIVING "FOR LIFE WITH MARY" </h1>
<br/>
Mary, by accepting your Scapular, I give myself to live in You and I desire to depend on You as completely as a child conceived from the mother in whom it lives. I want to live only your life like it. I want to think with your thoughts, want what you want and love Jesus with your love. I want to live in you and with you for His glory. Mary, I love you and joyfully give you my freedom, asking you to show me every day what I can make you happy. Amen.
