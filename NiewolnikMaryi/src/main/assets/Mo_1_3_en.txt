<h1> ACT OF DEDICATION TO THE SCAPLER MOTHER OF GOD <h1 />
<br/>
O most glorious Virgin Mary, Mother of the Holy Scapular! Here I am, unworthy of the honor of being called Your servant, emboldened by Your goodness and desire to serve You, I choose You as my only and best Mother and Advocate. Before the Most Holy Trinity, You, my Guardian Angel, I solemnly renew the promises I made to you, Most Holy Mother, on the day of receiving the Holy Scapular. I wish to faithfully and zealously serve you and your divine Son throughout my life. I also give you my filial thanks for having deigned to count me amongst so many millions of souls among your beloved children through the Holy Scapular. I promise You and I decide that I wish to remain your faithful son, love You with all my heart, and follow You in virtue and piety. Most Holy Mother, accept this sacrifice of mine and kindly grant me that the Holy Scapular that I am wearing becomes a sign of Your special protection, protection against sin, a shield against enemy attacks, an encouragement to practice virtue and a means to achieve salvation. Obtain this grace for me from Your Son Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.
