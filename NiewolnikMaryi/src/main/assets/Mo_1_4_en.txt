<h1>ACT OF DEDICATION TO THE IMMACULATE INITIATION<h1/>
<br/>
Mary Immaculate, I give myself to You as trustingly as God gave Himself to You. Together with you, I want Him - with His grace - to respond to His love for man. With your whole being, you give birth to Christ and identify us with Him, molding us in His image and likeness. Teach us daily, concrete adherence to the saving plan of your Son and lead us to union with Him. Heavenly Father himself, in Mary, you crushed Satan and created a "new man", so that he would overcome evil with the power of the Immaculate Conception. Let him triumph over the world, let it embrace every heart, so that it could trust in You as Mary entrusted. You, God in the Trinity. You gave yourself to the creature in the Holy Spirit to renew the face of the earth. Here I, with all creation, give myself to You in that Spirit, in the unfathomable mystery of the Immaculate Conception. I unite myself to the FIAT of Mary and with her, in her, through her and how I give you my life so that in it and through it you can fulfill your plan for the world, light my heart with the fire of the Holy Spirit and let all those you put on my way burn from it Amen.
