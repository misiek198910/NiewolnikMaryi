<h1> ACT OF CONCLUSION OF THE BVM FROM 2000 St. JOHN PAUL II<h1 />
<br/>
1. "Woman, behold your son!" (Jn 19:26). in whom you, O Mother, have offered us Jesus anew, the blessed fruit of your pure womb, the Incarnate Word, the Redeemer of the world, these words inspire us in a special way, because our thoughts turn to You and make You our Mother: "Woman, here is a son Yours! »By entrusting to You the Apostle John, with him the children of the Church and all men and women, Christ did not diminish, but rather reaffirmed His only Savior of the world. You are the brightness that does not obscure the light of Christ, because you exist in and through him. With your whole being you say fiat: You are Immaculate, in You the fullness of grace shines forth. And your children gather around you at the dawn of the new Voice of the Successor of Peter, where many shepherds are uniting, who have come here from all parts, the Church trustingly begs you for intercession today, resorting to your maternal protection When the link to the end of the Jubilee, Millennium Year . facing the challenges of the future.
<br/>
<br/>
2. This year, many have known grace and are still experiencing so much joy in the gift of mercy that the Father has given us to Christ. In the particular Churches scattered around, and especially here at the center of Christianity, many different groups of believers have accepted this gift. This place resonated with the enthusiasm of young people, hence the sick people carried their prayers. This way, priests and religious, artists and journalists, people of work and science, children and adults, all recognized in your beloved Son of God, made flesh in your womb. Through your intercession, obtain for us, O Mother, that the fruits of this Year may not be wasted, the seeds of grace may grow up to the full measure of holiness to which we are all called.
<br/>
<br/>
3. Today we want to entrust to you the future that awaits us, asking you to accompany us on our way. We live in an extraordinary epoch, thrilling and in full harmony. Today, mankind has at its disposal an incredibly modern parking lot, to spend the night in a flowering garden or to turn it into ruin. She has a remarkable ability to influence the same sources of life: she can accept them for good, within the limits of the moral law, but she can follow the voice of short-sighted pride that makes science reject options and even leads it to crush the respect due to every human being. Today, more than the potential of the past, humanity is at a crossroads. Also now, Most Holy Virgin, salvation is only and exclusively in your Son Jesus.
<br/>
<br/>
4. Therefore, Mother, we wish to take you to us, like the Apostle John (cf. Jn 19:27), to learn from you how to follow your Son. "Woman, behold your children!" We stand here before you to entrust ourselves, the Church and the whole world to your maternal care. Ask for us your beloved Son to give us abundantly the Holy Spirit, the Spirit of truth, who is the conspiratorial life. Receive him for us and with us, as in the first Jerusalem date gathered around you on the day of Pentecost (cf. Acts 1:14). Let the spirit open hearts to justice and love, may it lead peoples and nations to mutual understanding, and inspire them with a firm will for peace. We entrust to You all people, beginning with the weakest: children who have not yet been born into the world and those born of poverty and suffering, young people looking for meaning, people without jobs, plagued by hunger and disease. We entrust to you broken families, hopeless old people and all who are lonely and have no hope.
<br/>
<br/>
5. O Mother, who know the sufferings and hopes of the Church and the world, help your children in their daily trials, life spares no one, and make the darkness not prevailed over the darkness for all partners. To you, the Dawn of salvation, we entrust our journey in the new Millennium, so that under your guidance all people may find Christ, the light and the only Savior who reigns with the Father and the Holy Spirit for ever and ever. Amen.
