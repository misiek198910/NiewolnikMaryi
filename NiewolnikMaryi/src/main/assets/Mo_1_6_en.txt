<h1> ACT OF PERSONAL CONSECRATION TO THE IMMACULATE HEART OF MARY <h1 />
<br/>
O Mary, in Your Immaculate Heart created has its place, and heaven and earth admire the virtues and are filled with their fragrance. I, too, with admiration and reverence, enter today and forever into the Garden of Eden of Your Immaculate Heart, because you yourself encourage me to do so in these most advanced moments for the world. I am all yours - today and until the end of my days and for all eternity. You can deal with me as with the protruding of Your Garden: take me from here, even today, and transplant me to another place; water the little ones with water or touch the dryness of aridity and toil, surround with beautiful flowers, frown and friendly or thorns reluctant and hostile. You can give me an abundance of food or a barren soil of hunger, shortage and failure, and even cut me off from the root of life and health, because I know that in Your hands will be the best for me. I want to please everyone only you, O Mystical Rose, Rose of passion and glory, the most beautiful Flower of heaven and earth.
