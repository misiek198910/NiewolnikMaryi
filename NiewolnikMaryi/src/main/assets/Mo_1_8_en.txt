<h1> AVE MARIA <h1 />
<br/>
Hail, Mary, poor and humble woman, blessed by the Most High! Virgin of hope, a prophecy of new times, we join your hymn of praise, praise the Lord's mercy, proclaim the coming of the Kingdom and the complete liberation of man. Hail, Mary, humble Servant of the Lord, full of glory, Mother of Christ! Faithful Virgin, holy of the Tabernacle of the Word, teach us to persistently listen to the word, to be docile to the voice, sensitive to its call in the depths of our conscience of history and to the places of signs in events. Greetings, Mary, Sorrowful Woman, Living Mother! Virgin Bride at the foot of the cross, New Eve, be our guide on the paths of the world, teach us to live the love of Christ and spread it, teach us to abide with you at the countless crosses on which your Son is still crucified. Hail, Mary, woman of faith, first among the disciples! Virgin, Mother of the Church, help us always to be accountable to the hope in us, to trust in the goodness of man and in the Father's love. Teach us to build the world from within: in the depths of silence and prayer, in the joy of brotherly love, in the incomparable power of the cross. Holy Mary, Mother of believers, our Lady of Lourdes, pray for us. Amen.
