<h1> Day One - November 7 </h1>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Virgin, among women in the world no one was born like You, Daughter and Handmaid of the Most High King, Heavenly Father, Mother of the Most Holy Lord Jesus Christ, Spouse of the Holy Spirit: pray for us together with Saint. Michael the Archangel and with all the powers of heaven and all the saints to Your Most Holy, Beloved Son, Lord and Master.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Glory be to the Father.
<br/>
<br/>
We greet you, the most glorious Queen and Mother of Mercy, famous for the miracles in the image of the Gate of Dawn. We fall to our knees in front of Your Holy Image. We humbly beg You - enlighten our hearts with love and grant us the grace of persevering in the service of You and of Your Son, Jesus Christ.
<br/>
<br/>
We entrust to You, O Mother of Mercy and Mother of the Church, our entire diocese, its pastors, consecrated persons and all the faithful. Beg for us with your Son this grace, so that unity and faith may be strengthened among us for the good of the whole Church, that we may all, united by bonds of love, work selflessly in the vineyard of the Holy Church and build the Kingdom of God in human hearts.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Our Father. <br> <br/>
1x Hail Mary. <br> <br/>
1x Glory be to the Father. <br> <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Mother of Mercy - pray for us.
<br/>
<br/>
Most Merciful Mother you open your arms wide to us and you stretch out your hands full of all graces and gifts. Your motherly heart desires to give us all we need. Encouraged by this kindness, we turn to you with confidence. Pray for us, Mother, all that we need, and above all grant us your mighty mediation, that we may keep purity and innocence, faithfully and persistently cultivate children's love for You, and bear in our hearts the image of Your Son's Heart. May this heart protect us, guide us and lead us to the eternal light. Amen.
