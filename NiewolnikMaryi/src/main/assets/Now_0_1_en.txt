<h1> Day two - November 8 </h1>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Virgin, among women in the world no one was born like You, Daughter and Handmaid of the Most High King, Heavenly Father, Mother of the Most Holy Lord Jesus Christ, Spouse of the Holy Spirit: pray for us together with Saint. Michael the Archangel and with all the powers of heaven and all the saints to Your Most Holy, Beloved Son, Lord and Master.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Glory be to the Father.
<br/>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Mother of Mercy and Lady of the world, who in the Gate of Dawn you show great favors to all those who run to you, we greet you with the love of all creation, humbly asking you to obtain the gifts we need from your Son and lead us safely to heavenly glory.
<br/>
<br/>
O Mother of Mercy and Queen of Apostles, to You who are "priestly land", we entrust the bishops and priests of our diocese. Ask God for the favors they need, so that, constantly supported by the power and light of the Holy Spirit, they can safely lead God's Flock entrusted to them to the fullness of life and happiness. Mary, obtain for the students of the Radom Seminary so that, following you on the path of spiritual beauty, in your school they may learn evangelical life, God's love above all else, constant contemplation of the Word of God and zealous service to the brothers in the Vineyard of your Son Jesus Christ.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Our Father. <br> <br/>
1x Hail Mary. <br> <br/>
1x Glory be to the Father. <br> <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Mother of Mercy - pray for us.
