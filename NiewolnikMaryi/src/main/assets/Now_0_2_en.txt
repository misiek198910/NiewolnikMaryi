<h1> Day three - November 9 </h1>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Virgin, among women in the world no one was born like You, Daughter and Handmaid of the Most High King, Heavenly Father, Mother of the Most Holy Lord Jesus Christ, Spouse of the Holy Spirit: pray for us together with Saint. Michael the Archangel and with all the powers of heaven and all the saints to Your Most Holy, Beloved Son, Lord and Master.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Glory be to the Father.
<br/>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Mother of Mercy, who placed Your Holy Image in the Gate of Dawn, so that all in their needs may come to You as Mother and Guardian - we stand before You as children, humbly asking you to embrace us to Your Mother's Heart and commend us to Your beloved Son.
<br/>
<br/>
We give you, O Mother of Mercy and Queen of the family, our entire parish. Ask for an abundance of grace for the priests and sisters working here, and for all priests and religious from this parish. Look after all the faithful. May Christ, your Son, be a support and strength for the elderly and the lonely; for those bearing the cross of suffering - by strength and love; may he reign through his love and law in all families, being the source of their happiness, unity and strength; may, by influencing the hearts of children and young people with his grace, make them eager to follow the Gospel, so that they may become for one another apostles of goodness.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Our Father. <br> <br/>
1x Hail Mary. <br> <br/>
1x Glory be to the Father. <br> <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Mother of Mercy - pray for us.
