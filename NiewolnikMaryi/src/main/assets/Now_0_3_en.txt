<h1> Day Four - November 10 </h1>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Virgin, among women in the world no one was born like You, Daughter and Handmaid of the Most High King, Heavenly Father, Mother of the Most Holy Lord Jesus Christ, Spouse of the Holy Spirit: pray for us together with Saint. Michael the Archangel and with all the powers of heaven and all the saints to Your Most Holy, Beloved Son, Lord and Master.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Glory be to the Father.
<br/>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Mother of Mercy, who, through Your Holy Ostra Brama Image, you heal the sick, restore life to the dead - heal our sinful souls, so that with Your help we avoid eternal death and live with You and Your Son Jesus Christ in eternal happiness.
<br/>
<br/>
We entrust to You, O Mother of Mercy and Mother of God's grace, all the spiritual and material benefactors of our monastery, pray for them that God would grant them abundantly with His grace and truly Christian joy. Look, Mother, on those who built this temple and this monastery with the work of mind and hands, and obtain all graces and all blessings for the living, and eternal joy for the dead.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Our Father. <br> <br/>
1x Hail Mary. <br> <br/>
1x Glory be to the Father. <br> <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Mother of Mercy - pray for us.
