<h1> Day five - November 11 </h1>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Virgin, among women in the world no one was born like You, Daughter and Handmaid of the Most High King, Heavenly Father, Mother of the Most Holy Lord Jesus Christ, Spouse of the Holy Spirit: pray for us together with Saint. Michael the Archangel and with all the powers of heaven and all the saints to Your Most Holy, Beloved Son, Lord and Master.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Glory be to the Father.
<br/>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Mother of Mercy, a source of living water from which everyone can draw, help us with Your holy grace, defend against the dangers of the world and lead us happily to the port of salvation.
<br/>
<br/>
We give You, O Mother of Mercy and Help of Christians, all the dead from our diocese, parish, the entire Seraphic Order, our deceased parents, siblings, friends, relatives and benefactors. Ask God for their forgiveness and the grace of sharing in the joy of the saved, so that they may discover the unfathomable mysteries of God's love for all eternity.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Our Father. <br/>
1x Hail Mary. <br/>
1x Glory be to the Father. <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Mother of Mercy - pray for us.
