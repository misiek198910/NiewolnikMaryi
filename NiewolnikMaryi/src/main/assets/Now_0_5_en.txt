<h1> Day Six - November 12 </h1>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Virgin, among women in the world no one was born like You, Daughter and Handmaid of the Most High King, Heavenly Father, Mother of the Most Holy Lord Jesus Christ, Spouse of the Holy Spirit: pray for us together with Saint. Michael the Archangel and with all the powers of heaven and all the saints to Your Most Holy, Beloved Son, Lord and Master.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Glory be to the Father.
<br/>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Mother of Mercy and Lady of the whole world, who, through Your Holy Image, grant to all those who seek You countless graces, help us to live on earth, with our hearts in heaven, and by praising You, deserve to see You and reign eternally with You .
<br/>
<br/>
We entrust to You, Mother of Mercy and Comforter of the afflicted, all those who, entrusting themselves to God's love, entrusted themselves to our prayers. Obtain for them that they may receive forgiveness and joy, and their prayers may be answered in accordance with the Most Holy Will of God. By persevering in thanksgiving for the goods received, let them strengthen their faith and love, and let them experience the mystery of God's merciful presence in their lives ever more deeply.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Our Father. <br/>
1x Hail Mary. <br/>
1x Glory be to the Father. <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Mother of Mercy - pray for us.
