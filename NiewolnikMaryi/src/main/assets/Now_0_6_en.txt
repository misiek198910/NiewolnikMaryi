<h1> Day Seven- November 13 </h1>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Virgin, among women in the world no one was born like You, Daughter and Handmaid of the Most High King, Heavenly Father, Mother of the Most Holy Lord Jesus Christ, Spouse of the Holy Spirit: pray for us together with Saint. Michael the Archangel and with all the powers of heaven and all the saints to Your Most Holy, Beloved Son, Lord and Master.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Glory be to the Father.
<br/>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Mother of Mercy, gracious and generous in this Holy Image. We stand before you, confessing our weakness, sinfulness and imperfection, asking you to obtain for us from Your Son, our dearest Savior, Jesus Christ, the grace of faith, hope and love, that through You we may reach eternal happiness.
<br/>
<br/>
We commend you, O Mother of Mercy and Queen of the Seraphic Order, all three Orders of the Holy Father Francis. Ask your Son for this grace so that, faithful to the charism of your Founder, bringing peace and goodness to the world, they may show everyone how much love the Heavenly Father has given us and draw many to the path of holiness.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Our Father. <br/>
1x Hail Mary. <br/>
1x Glory be to the Father. <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Mother of Mercy - pray for us.
