<h1> Eighth day - November 14 </h1>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Virgin, among women in the world no one was born like You, Daughter and Handmaid of the Most High King, Heavenly Father, Mother of the Most Holy Lord Jesus Christ, Spouse of the Holy Spirit: pray for us together with Saint. Michael the Archangel and with all the powers of heaven and all the saints to Your Most Holy, Beloved Son, Lord and Master.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Glory be to the Father.
<br/>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Mother of Mercy, Comforter of the afflicted, we fall on our knees before Your Holy Image. To whom should we go in our sufferings, if not to you, Mother? We are drawn to you by the incomprehensible goodness of your Heart and the power of your intercession with your Son.
<br/>
<br/>
We recommend to you, O Mother of Mercy and Queen of the Seraphic Order, all the Poor Clares around the world. Let them continue to gaze at the example of the life of Saint Mother Clare. Help them always to adore and adore Jesus in the Blessed Sacrament, so that they may respond faithfully to the deep demands that flow from total love for Christ and from unreserved dedication to the mission of the Church.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Our Father. <br/>
1x Hail Mary. <br/>
1x Glory be to the Father. <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Mother of Mercy - pray for us.
