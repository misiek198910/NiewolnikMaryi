<h1> Ninth day - November 15 </h1>
<br/>
Holy Mary, Virgin, among women in the world no one was born like You, Daughter and Handmaid of the Most High King, Heavenly Father, Mother of the Most Holy Lord Jesus Christ, Spouse of the Holy Spirit: pray for us together with Saint. Michael the Archangel and with all the powers of heaven and all the saints to Your Most Holy, Beloved Son, Lord and Master.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Glory be to the Father.
<br/>
<br/>
O admirable Mother of our Savior, Immaculate Virgin, Mary of the Gate of Dawn, full of sweetness and gentleness. We fall to our knees before Your Holy Image and plead for Your mercy. Trusting in your goodness and kindness, we take refuge in Your protection, for in You the weak find strength and refreshment.
<br/>
<br/>
We entrust to You, O Mother of Mercy and Queen of the Seraphic Order, our religious community. Be always with us as our Mother and shape our hearts to yours. Ask your Son for us to let girls come to our convent who wish to lead a life of poverty and seraphic love with us. May we become luminous models for them, to attract them to the service of God and that we may sanctify ourselves together in sisterly love, resting on your motherly love.
<br/>
<br/>
O Mary, Spouse of the Holy Spirit, ask for us that, looking at your example, following the example of the saints and blessed of our Order, we may share more and more in the mysteries of salvation, remain with God with our whole mind and heart, grow in docility to the Holy Spirit and more fully realize His gifts in our lives.
<br/>
<br/>
1x Our Father. <br/>
1x Hail Mary. <br/>
1x Glory be to the Father. <br/>
<br/>
<br/>
Mother of Mercy - pray for us.
