<br/><br/><h3> September 12 - Jakov's year-long revelation </h3>
Jakov Colo (pronounced Czolo) had daily apparitions from June 25, 1981 to September 12, 1998. On that day, Our Lady said:
"Dear child! I am your mother and I love you without any conditions. From now on, I will not be revealing myself to you every day, but only at Christmas, the day of my Son's birth. Do not be sad because as a mother I will always be with you and like any real mother I will never leave you. Continue on the path of my Son, the path of peace and love, and try to persevere in the message that I have entrusted to you. Be an example of a man who has known God and God's love. Let people always see in you an example of how God works on people and how God works through people. I bless you with my motherly blessing and thank you for having responded to my call. "
After telling him the tenth secret, she told him that for the rest of his life he would have a revelation once a year at Christmas.

<br/><br/><h3> December 25th - Jakov's year-long revelation </h3>
After the apparition, which began at 11:50 am and lasted 12 minutes, Jakov wrote: "Our Lady came joyful. She greeted me as usual with the words: 'Praise be to Jesus Christ.' She spoke about the secrets and then gave this message to me:"
"Dear children! Today, on the birth of my Son, my heart is filled with immeasurable joy, love and peace. And as your Mother, I want each of you to feel the same joy, peace and love in your heart. Therefore, do not be afraid to open your heart and give yourself completely to Jesus, because only in this way can He enter your heart and fill it with love, peace and joy. I bless you with my motherly blessing. "
Jakov prayed with his family. He prepared himself for the revelation through confession and Holy Mass. After the apparition, he cried for a while.
