<br/><br/><h3>25 January</h3>
Dear children! I am calling you at this time to prayer, fasting and renunciation, that you may be stronger in faith.This is a time of awakening and of giving birth. As nature which gives itself, you also, little children, ponder how much you have received. Be joyful bearers of peace and love that it may be good for you on earth. Yearn for Heaven; and in Heaven there is no sorrow or hatred. That is why, little children, decide anew for conversion and let holiness begin to reign in your life. Thank you for having responded to my call. ”

<br/><br/><h3>25 February</h3>
Dear children! God has permitted me to be with you also today, to call you to prayer and fasting. Live this time of grace and be witnesses of hope, because I repeat to you, little children, that with prayer and fasting also wars can be suppressed. Little children, believe and live this time of grace in faith and with faith; and my Immaculate Heart does not leave any of you in peacelessness if you have recourse to me. I intercede for you before the Most High and I pray for peace in your hearts and for hope for the future. Thank you for having responded to my call. ”

<br/><br/><h3> March 18 </h3>
"Dear children! Like a Mother, I call you to return to the joy and truth of the Gospel, to return to the love of my Son, because He is waiting for you with open arms, so that everything you do in life, you would do with my Son, with love, that it might be blessed, that your spirituality was deep, not just superficial. Only in this way will you be humble and generous, filled with love and joyful. And my motherly heart will rejoice with you. Thank you. "

<br/><br/><h3> March 25 </h3>
"Dear children! Also today I am with you to tell you: little children, who prays is not afraid of the future and does not lose hope. You are chosen to bring joy and peace because you belong to me. I have come here as the Queen of Peace because the devil wants unrest and war, he wants to fill your heart with fear for the future, and the future belongs to God. Therefore, be humble, pray and entrust everything into the hands of the Most High who created you. Thank you for having responded to my call. "

<br/><br/><h3> April 25 </h3>
"Dear children! Message, Today I am calling you to bear witness to your faith in the colors of spring. Let it be a faith of hope and courage. Little children, may your faith be steadfast in any situation, even at this time of temptation. Boldly walk with the Risen Christ towards heaven, which is your goal. I accompany you on this path of holiness and I place you all in my Immaculate Heart. Thank you for having responded to my call. "

<br/><br/><h3> May 25 </h3>
"Dear children! I look at you and I am calling you: come back to God, because he is love and he sent me to you out of love to lead you on the path of conversion. Leave sin and evil, decide for holiness and there will be joy, and you will be my outstretched hands in this lost world. I want you to be a prayer and hope for those who have not come to know the God of love. Thank you for having responded to my call. "

<br/><br/><h3> June 25 </h3>
"Dear children! My heart is joyful because over the years I see your love and openness to my call. Today I am calling you all: pray with me for peace and freedom, because Satan is strong and through his deceptions he wants to lead as many hearts as possible away from my motherly heart. Therefore, decide for God so that you may be well in the land that God has given you. Thank you for having responded to my call. "

<br/><br/><h3> July 25 </h3>
"Dear children! I am calling you to be a prayer for all those who do not pray. With your lives, little children, witness to your joy that you belong to me, and God will hear your prayers and give you peace in this troubled world where pride and selfishness reign. Little children, be generous and [be] the love of my love, that the pagans feel that you belong to me and that they turn to my Immaculate Heart. Thank you for having responded to my call. "

<br/><br/><h3> August 25 </h3>
"Dear children! Little children, with joy I call all of you who have responded to my call, be joy and peace. By your life [bear] the witness of heaven that I bear to you. It is time, little children, for you to be a reflection of my love for all those who do not love and whose hearts are filled with hatred. Do not forget: I am with you and I intercede for you all before my Son Jesus, that He may give you His peace. Thank you for having responded to my call. "

<br/><br/><h3> September 25 </h3>
"Dear children! Pray, [bear] witness and rejoice with me, for the Most High continues to send me to lead you on the way of holiness. Little children, be aware that life is short and that eternity awaits you, so that you, with all the saints, glorify God with your existence. Little children, do not worry about earthly things, but long for Heaven. Heaven will be your goal and joy will reign in your heart. I am with you and I bless you all with my motherly blessing. Thank you for having responded to my call. "

<br/><br/><h3> October 25 </h3>
"Dear children! Return to prayer because [he] who prays is not afraid of the future. [the one] Whoever prays is open to life and respects the lives of others. Little children, [one] who prays feels the freedom of God's children and with joy in his heart serves for the good of man's brother. Because God is love and freedom. Therefore, little children, when they want to put you in chains and use you, it is not from God, because God is love and He gives His peace to every creature. That is why he sent me to help you, so that you would grow on the way of holiness. Thank you for having responded to my call. "

<br/><br/><h3> November 25 </h3>
"Dear children! I am with you at this time of grace and I call you all to bring peace and love to this world, where God calls you, little children, through me, to be prayer and love and the embodiment of Paradise here on earth. May your hearts be filled with joy and faith in God, so that you, little children, put your full trust in His holy will. That is why I am with you, because He - the Most High - brings me among you, that I may awaken hope in you and that you may be intercessors of peace in this troubled world. Thank you for having responded to my call. "

<br/><br/><h3> December 25 </h3>
"Dear children! Today I am bringing my Son Jesus to you so that He may give you His peace. Little children, without peace you have no future or blessing, so come back to prayer, because the fruit of prayer is joy and faith without which you cannot live. The blessing that we give you today, bring to your families and make all [people] whom you meet so that they feel the grace you receive. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
