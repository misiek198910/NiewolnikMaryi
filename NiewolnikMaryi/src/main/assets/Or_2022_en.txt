<br/><br/><h3> January 25 </h3>
"Dear children! Today I invite you to return to personal prayer. Little children, do not forget that Satan is strong and that he wants to attract as many souls as possible to him. Therefore, stay awake in prayer and be determined for what is good. I am with you and I bless you all with my motherly blessing. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3> February 25 </h3>
"Dear children! I am with you and we pray together. Help me, little children, by praying that Satan may not gain the upper hand. His power of death, hatred and fear has come upon Earth. Therefore, little children, come back to God and pray, fast and renounce for all those who are trampled, poor and without a voice in this world without God. Little children, if you do not return to God and His commandments, you have no future. That is why he sent me to you to guide you. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3> March 18 </h3>
“Dear children, with my motherly love I am calling you to look to my Son, full of strength, faith and trust. Open your hearts to him and do not be afraid. For my Son is the Light of the World and in Him there is peace and hope. Therefore, I am calling you again to pray for these children of mine who did not know the love of my Son; that my Son may enlighten their hearts with his light of love and hope, and that my children may give you courage, peace and hope. I am with you, thank you. "
<br/><br/><h3> March 25 </h3>
"Dear children! I hear your cries and prayers for peace. Satan has been fighting for war for years. That is why God sent me to you to lead you on the path of holiness, because humanity is at a crossroads. I am calling you to come back to God and to God's commandments, so that you may be well on Earth and that you come out of this crisis that you have entered, because you do not listen to God who loves you and wants to save you and lead you to a new life. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3> April 25 </h3>
"Dear children! I look at you and see that you are lost. That is why I am calling you all: return to God, return to prayer, and the Holy Spirit will fill you with His love that gives joy to your heart. Hope will also grow in you for a better future, and you will become joyful witnesses of God's mercy in you and around you. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3> May 25 </h3>
"Dear children! I look at you and I thank God for each of you, because He allows me to be with you still, to persuade you to holiness. Little children, peace is disturbed and Satan wants restlessness. Therefore, let your prayer be even stronger that every unclean spirit of division and war will be silenced. Be builders of peace and bring the joy of the Risen One in you and around you, so that good will prevail in every person. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3> June 25 </h3>
"Dear children! I rejoice with you and thank you for every sacrifice and prayer that you have offered for my intentions. Little children, do not forget that you are important in my plan for the salvation of mankind. Come back to God and to pray that the Holy Spirit may work in you and through you. Little children, I am with you also in these days when Satan is fighting for war and hatred. The divisions are deep and evil works in man like never before. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3> July 25 </h3>
"Dear children! I am with you to lead you on the path of conversion, because, little children, through your life you can bring many souls closer to my Son. Be joyful witnesses of the Word of God and love, with hope in your heart that overcomes all evil. Forgive those who do evil to you and follow the path of holiness. I am leading you to my Son so that He may be for you the Way, the Truth and the Life. Thank you for having responded to my call. " <br/><br/><h3>August 25</h3>“Dear children! God allows me to be with you and to lead you along the path of peace, so that through peace in yourselves you may build peace in the world. I am with you and I intercede for you before my Son Jesus, that he may give you firm faith and hope for a better future that I desire to build with you. Be brave and do not be afraid, for God is with you. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>September 25</h3>
"Dear children! Pray that the Holy Spirit will give you light, that you may joyfully seek God and be witnesses of boundless love. Little children, I am with you and I am calling all of you again: gather your courage and bear witness to the good works that God is doing in you and through you. Be joyful in God. Do good to your neighbor so that you may be well on earth and pray for peace, which is in danger because Satan wants war and unrest. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>October 25</h3>
"Dear children! The Most High allowed me to be with you and to be a joy and a path of hope for you, because mankind has decided to die. That is why he sent me to teach you that without God you have no future. Little children, be instruments of love for all those who have not come to know the God of love. Give joyful witness to your faith and do not lose hope for the transformation of the human heart. I am with you and I bless you with my motherly blessing. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>25 November</h3>
“Dear children! The Most High has sent me to you to teach you prayer. Prayer opens hearts and gives hope, and faith is born and strengthened. Little children, I call you with love, return to God, because God is love and your hope. You have no future if you do not decide for God and that is why I am with you to guide you to choose conversion and life, not death. Thank you for responding to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>25 December Jakov</h3>
“Dear children! Today, when the light of Jesus' birth illuminates the whole world, with Jesus in my arms I pray in a special way that every heart will become a stable in Bethlehem in which my Son will be born and that your life will become the light of His birth. Little children, you live in anxiety and fear. Therefore, little children, today, on this day of grace, ask Jesus to strengthen your faith and become the master of your lives, because, my children, only with Jesus in your life will you not see anxiety, but pray for peace and live in peace and you will not notice fear, but Jesus who frees us from all fears. I am your mother who constantly watches over you and I bless you with my motherly blessing. "
<br/><br/><h3>25 December </h3>
“Dear children! Today I bring you my son Jesus so that you may be His peace and a reflection of the serenity and joy of Heaven. Little children, pray that you may be open to receiving peace, because many hearts are closed to the call of light that changes hearts. I am with you and I pray for you that you may open yourselves to receiving the King of Peace who fills your hearts with warmth and blessing. Thank you for responding to my call. "
