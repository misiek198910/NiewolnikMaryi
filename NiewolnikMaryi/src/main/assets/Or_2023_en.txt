<br/><br/><h3> January 25</h3>
"Dear children! Pray with me for peace, because Satan wants war and hatred in hearts and nations. Therefore, pray and sacrifice your days through fasting and penance, that God may give you peace. The future is at a crossroads because modern man does not want God. That's why humanity is going downhill. You, little children, are my hope. Pray with me that what I started in Fatima and here will come true. Pray and bear witness to peace in your surroundings and be people of peace. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3> February 25</h3>
"Dear children! Convert and put on penitential garments and deep personal prayer and humbly ask the Almighty for peace. In this time of grace, Satan wants to seduce you, but you, little children, look at my Son and follow Him to Calvary through renunciation and fasting. I am with you because the Almighty allows me to love you and lead you to joy of heart, in a faith that grows for all those who love God above all else. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3> March 18</h3>
“Dear children, I am calling you to get to know my Son as best as possible through prayer and mercy. That you may learn to listen with a pure and open heart. That you listen to what my Son tells you, that you see spiritually. That you, as one nation of God in union with my Son, may bear witness to the truth with your lives. Pray, my children, that together with my Son you may bring only peace, joy and love to all your brothers and sisters. I am with you and I bless you with a motherly blessing. "
<br/><br/><h3>March 25</h3>
"Dear children! May this time be a time of prayer for you. "
<br/><br/><h3> April 25</h3>
"Dear children! I call you all to bring the peace and joy of the risen Jesus to all those who are far from prayer, so that the love of Jesus, through your lives, may transform them into a new life of conversion and holiness. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>May 25</h3>
"Dear children! I am calling you to go to the bosom of nature and pray that the Most High speaks to your heart and that you feel the power of the Holy Spirit, that you bear witness to the love that God has for all creation. I am with you and I intercede for you. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>June 25</h3>
"Dear children! The Most High allowed me to be among you, to pray for you, to be a mother and shelter for you. Little children, I am calling you, return to God and to prayer and God will bless you in full. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>July 25</h3>
"Dear children! In this time of grace, in which the Most High sends me to you to love you and lead you on the path of conversion, offer your prayers and sacrifices for all those who are far away and have not come to know God's love. Little children, be witnesses of love and peace for all troubled hearts. Thank you for having responded to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>August 25</h3>
“Dear children! In this time of grace, I call you to pray with your heart. Little children, may your hearts be raised in prayer towards heaven, so that your heart may feel the God of love who heals you and loves you with immeasurable love. That is why I am with you, so that I may lead you on the path of conversion of heart. Thank you for responding to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>September 25</h3>
<br>Dear children! I call you to intense prayer. Modernism wants to penetrate your thoughts and steal the joy of prayer and meeting Jesus. My dear children, renew prayer in your families, so that my motherly heart may be as joyful as in the first days when I chose you, and the prayer will continue day and night, and heaven will not be silent, but will abundantly give peace and blessing to this place. Thank you for responding to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>October 25</h3>
“Dear children, the winds of evil, hatred and anxiety are blowing on the earth to annihilate the lives of [many people]. That is why the Most High has sent me to you to lead you to the path of peace and unity with God and people. Little children, you are my outstretched hands: pray, fast and make sacrifices for peace. [Peace is] The treasure that every heart desires. Thank you for responding to my call. "
<br/><br/><h3>November 25</h3>
“Dear children, may this time be interspersed with prayer for peace and good deeds, so that you may have the joy of waiting [for] the King of Peace in your hearts, families and in a world that lacks hope. Thank you for responding to my call. "
