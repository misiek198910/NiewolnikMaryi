<br/><br/><h3> January 25 </h3>
“Dear children! Let this be a time of prayer.”
<br/><br/><h3> February 25 </h3>
“Dear children! Pray and renew your hearts, so that the good you have sown may bear fruit of joy and unity with God. Weeds have taken over many hearts and they have become barren. Therefore, little children, be light, love, and my outstretched hands in a world that longs for God, who is love. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> March 18 </h3>
Visionary Mirjana Dragićević-Soldo had daily apparitions from June 24, 1981, to December 25, 1982. During the last apparition, the Blessed Mother entrusted her with the 10th secret and told her she would appear to her once a year, on March 18. So it has been until today—also this year. Several thousand pilgrims gathered for the Rosary prayer. The apparition began at 13:23 and ended at 13:27.
Dear children, through the merciful love of God, I am with you. And therefore, as a mother, I call you to believe in love. Love that is union with my Son. Through love, you help others to open their hearts, to get to know my Son and to love Him. My children, love allows my Son to enlighten your hearts with His grace, to grow within you, and to grant you peace. My children, if you live love, if you live my Son, you will have peace and you will be happy. In love there is victory. Thank you!”
<br/><br/><h3> March 25 </h3>
“Dear children! In this time of grace, pray with me so that goodness may triumph in and around you. Little children, in a special way, pray in union with Jesus on His Way of the Cross. Include in your prayers all of humanity, which is wandering without God and His love. Little children, be prayer, be light, and be witnesses to all those you meet, so that the merciful God may have mercy on you. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> April 25 </h3>
“Dear children! I am with you to tell you that I love you and to encourage you to pray, because Satan is strong and his power is growing every day through those who have chosen death and hatred. Little children, be prayer and my outstretched hands of love for all those who are in darkness and seeking the light of our God. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> May 25 </h3>
“Dear children! In this time of grace, I call you to heartfelt prayer. Little children, form prayer groups—groups where you will encourage one another to do good and grow in joy. Little children, you are still far away. That is why I call you again and again to conversion and to choose the path of holiness and hope, so that God may grant you the fullness of peace. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> June 25 </h3>
“Dear children! I rejoice with you and thank God for allowing me to be with you, to lead you, and to love you. Little children, peace is threatened and the family is under attack. Little children, I call you to return to prayer in the family. Place the Holy Bible in a visible place and read it every day. Love God above all things so that it may go well for you on earth. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> July 25 </h3>
“My dear children! I have chosen you with joy and I am leading you, because I see in you, little children, people of faith, hope, and prayer. Let your pride be that you belong to me, and I am leading you to Him who is the Way, the Truth, and the Life. I am with you so that peace may triumph in you and around you, for it is with this intention that God has sent me to you. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> August 25 </h3>
“Dear children! Today I pray with you for peace. Good and evil are fighting to take control of the world and the hearts of people. Be people of hope and prayer and of great trust in God the Creator, for whom all things are possible. Little children, let peace reign in you and around you. I bless you with my motherly blessing so that you, little children, may be a joy to all those you meet. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> September 25 </h3>
“Dear children! Out of love for you, God has sent me to you to love you and to encourage you to prayer and conversion, so that you may receive peace in yourselves, in your families, and in the world. Little children, do not forget that true peace comes only through prayer from God, who is your peace. Thank you for having responded to my call.” (With Church approval)
<br/><br/><h3> October 25 </h3>
“Dear children! In this time when you celebrate All Saints' Day, pray for their intercession and prayers so that, in unity with them, you may find peace. Let the saints be your intercessors and examples, so that you may follow them and live in holiness. I am with you and I intercede before God for each one of you. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> November 25 </h3>
“Dear children! In this grace-filled time of waiting, I desire to call you to prayer, so that Advent may be a time of family prayer. Little children, I gently embrace you and, in a special way, encourage you to pray for peace in the world, so that peace may overcome restlessness and hatred. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> December 25 </h3>
“Dear children, today, on this day of grace, I especially call you not to live your life focused on earthly goals and not to seek peace and joy in worldly things, because that way your life is overtaken by darkness and you do not see the meaning of your life. Little children, open the doors of your hearts to Jesus, allow Him to take over your entire life, so that you may begin to live in God’s love and mercy. My children, only with Jesus in your hearts will you discover the true purpose of your life and strive for eternal salvation. I bless you with my motherly blessing.”
