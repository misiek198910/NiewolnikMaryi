<br/><br/><h3> January 25 </h3>
“Dear children! In this year of grace, I call you to conversion. Dear children, place God at the center of your life, and the fruits will be love for your neighbor and the joy of witnessing; the holiness of your life will become a true testimony of faith. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> February 25 </h3>
“Dear children! Let this springtime be an incentive for your personal conversion, so that through your life you may pray and love God above all things, for all those in need. Little children, be my hands of peace and prayer, be love for all those who do not love, do not pray, and do not seek peace. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> March 18 </h3>
“Dear children! With motherly love, I ask you: give me your hands joined in prayer, give me your hearts cleansed through confession, and I will lead you to my Son. Because, dear children, only my Son can illuminate the darkness with His light, only He can remove suffering with His word. Therefore, do not be afraid to come with me, for I am leading you to my Son, to salvation. Thank you.”
<br/><br/><h3> March 25 </h3>
“Dear children! In this time of grace, when you are called to conversion, I encourage you, little children, to offer me your prayers, sufferings, and tears for the conversion of hearts that are far from the heart of my Son Jesus. Little children, pray with me, because without God you have no future and no eternal life. I love you, but without you I cannot help you. Therefore, say ‘yes’ to God. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> April 25 </h3>
“Dear children! Restless winds of selfishness and sin are overtaking many hearts and leading them to unrest and downfall. Little children, that is why I call you: return to God in prayer, so that goodness may dwell in your hearts and in the land where you live. Little children, I love you and that is why I tirelessly call you to conversion. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
<br/><br/><h3> May 25 </h3>
“Dear children! In this time of grace, I call you to be people of hope, peace, and joy, so that every person may be a bearer of peace and a lover of life. Little children, ask the Holy Spirit to fill you with the strength of the Holy Spirit, courage, and trust. Then this time will be a gift for you and a journey in holiness toward eternal life. I am with you and I love you. Thank you for having responded to my call.”
