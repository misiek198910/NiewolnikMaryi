5. (cont.) Fire tests hard iron (Ekli 31:31), and a righteous man in temptations. Often we don't know what we can, but temptation reveals what we are. And one should watch out especially at the beginning of temptation, because it is easier to defeat the enemy when he is prevented by all means from having access to his soul and he is blocking his way when he begins to knock at the gate. Hence it was said: deal with evil in the beginning; you use medication in vain when your illness has worsened over a long delay. (Ovid, Book of Medicines 91). For first there is a simple thought, then a vivid image, then a liking, then a bad heart move, then consent. So slowly does the wicked enemy come in when he is not resisted at first. The longer one delays with internal resistance to temptation, the weaker the day becomes, and the enemy becomes stronger and stronger <br> </br>
6. Some will experience more severe temptations at the beginning of their conversion, others at the end. Some people are troubled almost all their lives. Still others are visited hardly ever and not too painfully; and all this happens according to the wisdom and justice of God, which takes into account the condition and merits of every person, and all leads to the salvation of the elect. <br> </br>
7. Therefore, we should not despair in temptations, but all the more earnestly ask God to support us in every tribulation. He for sure according to words of St. Paul, he will not allow you to be tempted beyond what you can, but with temptation he will also prepare a way out so that you may endure (1 Cor 10:13). Therefore, humble yourselves in every affliction and temptation under the mighty hand of God (1 Peter 5: 6), for He will save and exalt the humble in spirit. <br> </br>
8. In temptations and torments, a person experiences what he has done; then he also acquires greater merit and his virtue is revealed more. What is great when a person is pious and zealous, when he is okay? However, if one is patient in times of adversity, then there is hope of making good progress. Some are able to resist great temptations, yet succumb to the small and the everyday; God permits it, that so humiliated, they do not trust themselves too much during great trials, since under the weight of small ones they fall. <br> </br>

<h3> Veni, Creator Spiritus </h3>
<br/>
1. O Creator, Spirit, come down, lead the hearts of the faithful to You circle, send Heavenly grace to your breasts, which are the work of your hands. <br> </br>
2. You are called the Comforter, You are the gift of Almighty God, You are the anointing of our souls, the living spring, love, ardor of fire. <br> </br>
3. You are grace sevenfold, for you have strength from the right hand of the Father, promised by the Father, you enrich our language with speech. <br> </br>
4. Illuminate our thoughts with light, pour holy love into our hearts, And confirm the weak weakness of our bodies with the constancy of your strength. <br> </br>
5. Drive the enemy out, and give peace for everlasting time. Under your steadfast leadership, let all harm be avoided. <br> </br>
6. Let us know the Father through You, let the Son also be known, And let us, the Spirit of both of them, believe in You with all our might. <br> </br>
7. Be to God the Father, glory, honor, the Son who raised from the dead, And to the One who comforts us, for eternity whose end is a stranger. <br> </br>
Amen.

Hail sea, fiery Star, Mother of God, Virgin always pure, heaven's gate blessed.
Gabryela: “Ave” being welcomed, return to us kind days, changing the name of Eve. Take off the fetters of prisoners, light the fires of the blind, remove the pain of life, ask for all the gifts.
Be our Mother, defend God in heaven, who wanted to be born of you through great love. Virgin peculiar, meek before all, break the links of sins, make us meek.

Glory be to God the Father, worship the power of Christ, let one eternal honor burn with the Three Spirit! Amen.

<h3> Magnificat - Hymn of thanksgiving of the Blessed Virgin Mary </h3>
<br/>
My soul magnifies the Lord, and my spirit rejoices in God my Savior. For he has considered the lowness of his handmaid;
For, from now on, all nations will call me blessed. Mighty has done great things for me, and holy is his name.
And his mercy is from all to all those who fear him.
He has made strength his arm, he has scattered the proud in their hearts. He threw down the mighty from the capital and raised up the lowly.
He has filled the hungry with good, and let the rich go empty.
He welcomed Israel his servant, remembering his mercy.
As he spoke to our fathers, to Abraham and his descendants for ever.
