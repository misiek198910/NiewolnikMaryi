<h3> "Mary's Secret" St. Ludwik Grignon de Montfort, Par. 23 - 29 </h3>
<br/>
23. The whole difficulty is to really and truly find the Most Holy Virgin Mary in order to be able to find all the most wonderful graces. God, being Master over teacher, can give directly through Himself what is usually only given through Mary. We would even be in a hurry to deny that she does so sometimes. Nevertheless, St. Thomas teaches us that, in harmony with the order of grace established by God's Wisdom, God usually communicates with man only through Mary. Therefore, if we want to ascend to Him and be united with Him, we must use the same path that He came down to us to become human and grant us His graces. Here is the meaning of a true devotion to Our Blessed Lady. <br> </br>
24. We know a whole range of true devotions to Our Lady, not to mention false ones. <br> </br>
25. The first true devotion we mention consists of fulfilling our Christian duties, such as avoiding mortal sin, acting more out of love than fear, praying to Our Lady, and paying homage to her as the Mother of God, but not specifically to her. devotions. <br> </br>
26 The second type of devotion is that we bestow Our Lady with greater feelings of appreciation and love, trust and adoration. This leads us to the Rosary Rings of St. and the Scapular, for the recitation of the five or fifteen mysteries of the Holy Rosary, for paying homage to Mary in her images and altars, for proclaiming her glory and belonging to the organization of her name. This devotion is good, holy, and commendable, as long as we know how to remain free from
sin. And it is not as perfect as the one I am about to talk about, not so good in making our soul independent of others and ourselves in order to be united with Jesus Christ. <br> </br>
27. The third gives rise to devotion to Our Lady, known and practiced by very few, is the one I want to tell you, chosen souls, now. <br> </br>
28. It consists in giving ourselves completely both as a slave to Mary and to Jesus through Mary, and then doing everything we do, through Mary, with Mary, in Mary and for Mary. <br> </br>
29. We should choose some special feast in which we dedicate ourselves, offer ourselves, and consecrate ourselves to Mary entirely voluntarily, with love and without any compulsion: our body and soul, everything we have, home, family and property; also our whole interior and spiritual values: our merits, graces, virtues and joys. Let us note that in this type of service the soul dedicates to Jesus through Mary everything that is most precious to her, things that no law requires to sacrifice, namely the right to dispose of oneself, the value of our prayers and victims, our mortifications and joys. The soul voluntarily puts everything at the disposal of Our Lady, thus She can dispose of us in accordance with Her will for the greater glory of God, in which She herself has the best discernment. <br> </br>

<h3>Prayer to Mary</h3>
<b>St. Ludwik Maria Grignon de Montfort, translated by Magdalena Tuszyńska from an English text</b>
<br/>
<br/>
Hail Mary, beloved daughter of the Eternal Father! Hail Mary, glorified Mother of the Son!
Hail Mary, faithful Bride of the Holy Spirit!
Hail Mary, my beloved Mother, beloved Lord, my all-powerful Queen!
Hail my joy, my glory, my heart and my soul!
You are all mine thanks to mercy, and I am all thanks to justice.
But I'm not completely yours yet. So now I devote myself to You completely, keeping nothing for myself or for others. And if you still see something in me that doesn't belong to you, I am begging you, remove it,
and make Yourself the absolute ruler of all that is mine.
Destroy in me everything that can hurt God, erode and eliminate
plant in me and cultivate everything that pleases You.
Let the light of faith disperse the darkness of my mind, let deep humility take the place of my pride, let high reflection enter the place of my wandering imagination, let the image of God fill my memory with His presence, let the fiery love of your heart ignite my coldness, let Your virtues take the place my sins, may Your merits be the only decoration in God's eyes and give me everything that is expected of me.
Please, beloved Mother, if it is possible, that I would have no other spirit than yours, that I would know Jesus and His God's Will; that I would not have a soul other than yours to worship and praise God, that I would not have a heart other than yours, to love God with a love as pure and burning as yours.
I am not asking you for visions, revelations, special devotions or spiritual satisfaction.
Your privilege is that you see God very clearly
Your privilege is to enjoy heavenly pleasure,
Your privilege is to triumph gloriously in Heaven at the Son's right hand
And to wield absolute power over Angels, people and demons.
Your privilege is to dispose of all God's gifts as you wish.
This, heavenly Mary, is the "best part" that the Lord has given you and which will never be taken away from You. And this thought fills my heart with joy.
And as for my part here on earth, I am asking for nothing but what is yours: to believe deeply without spiritual joys, to suffer joyfully without the comfort of people,
to die constantly for yourself, without a respite, and to work earnestly and unselfishly until You die, as the most humble servant among Your servants.
The only grace for which I am begging you to receive for me is that I may, every day and in
every moment of my life he could say:
Amen, let it be so - in front of all that you did while you were still on earth; Amen, let it be so - in front of all that you do now in Heaven;
Amen, so be it - in front of all that you do in my soul,
that you alone could glorify Jesus in me, in time and in eternity. Amen.
