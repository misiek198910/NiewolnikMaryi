<h3> Treatise on the Perfect Devotion to the Blessed Virgin Mary, Part II, 1 </h3>
<br/>
Jesus Christ, our Savior, true God and true Man, must be the final end of all our services; otherwise it would be wrong and deceptive. Jesus Christ is Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end of everything. We only work, as the Apostle said, to make every man perfect in Jesus Christ, because only in Him dwells all the wholeness of Godhead and all the fullness of grace, virtue and perfection; for only in Him have we received the fullness of spiritual blessing, for He is our only Master to teach us, the only Lord on whom we depend, the only Head with whom we are to be connected, the only Model to be conformed to, our only Physician who He is to heal us, the only Shepherd to feed us, the only Way to guide us, the only Truth to believe, the only Life to animate us; in a word, it is everything to us in everything.
For there has been no other name under heaven by which we should be saved; God has laid no other foundation for our salvation, for our perfection, and for our glory, than Jesus Christ. Any edifice not resting on this rock stands on flying sand and will fall sooner or later. Every faithful one who does not remain in him like a branch with a vine will fall off and wither, and be worthy to be thrown into the fire. Apart from Him, everything is pathless, false, unrighteous, death and damnation. But if we are in Jesus Christ and Jesus Christ in us, we need not fear condemnation. Neither the angels in Heaven, nor the people on earth, nor Satans in Hell, nor any other creature can hurt us, because they cannot separate us from
God's love that is in Jesus Christ. Through him, with him and in him, we can do everything: we can give back to God the Father in the unity of the Holy Spirit. all honor and glory (Canon of the Mass), we can become perfect, and for our neighbor the good fragrance of Christ for eternal life. (Cf. 2 Cor 2: 15-16).
Therefore, if we preach perfect devotion to the Blessed Virgin, it is only to make our devotion to Jesus Christ more thorough and perfect, and to provide an easy and sure means of finding Christ. If devotion to the Blessed Virgin would distance us from Jesus Christ, it would have to be rejected as a satanic illusion. But it is quite the opposite ... This devotion is necessary, but only in order to find Jesus Christ completely, love Him and serve Him faithfully.
I turn to You, my beloved Jesus, to lament Your Majesty that most Christians, even among the most learned, do not understand the necessary connection that exists between You and Your holy Mother. You, Lord, are always with Mary, and Mary is always with You, and she cannot be without You; otherwise it would cease to be what it is. Grace transformed her into you so that she is no longer alive and she is not, but you, my Jesus, live and reign in her, more perfect than in all the angels and blessed. Ah, if we knew the glory and the love you receive in this wonderful being, verily, different for You and for Her, we would have feelings. Mary is so closely united with you that it would be easier to separate light and warmth from fire, and even - let me say boldly, it would be sooner to separate all the angels and saints from you than your blessed mother, because she loves you more ardently and glorifies you better than all other creatures together.

<h3>Prayer to Jesus Christ</h3>
<b>St. Ludwik Maria Grignon de Montfort - translated by Magdalena Tuszyńska, from an English text</b>
<br/>
<br/>
O beloved Jesus, allow me to bow deeply to You, thank you for the grace You have given me, giving me Your Holy Mother through the service of Holy Slavery.
Thanks to this, she can be my lawyer before Your Majesty and support in my infinite misery. OH lord I am so devastated that without this beloved Mother I would inevitably be lost.
Yes, I need Mary by your side and all around me: that she would alleviate your righteous anger,
because I have been against you so often; that she would save me from the eternal punishment of Your righteousness which I owe rightly; so that she could ponder you, address you
to pray to you and to appease you; that she could help me save my soul and the souls of others; In a word, I need Mary, so that I can always do Your Holy Will and strive for Your greatest glory in everything.
Ah, if I could spread the mercy you have shown to me all over the world!
That everyone would know that if it wasn't for Mary, I would be condemned to perpetual damnation!
If I could offer the due thank you for such a great blessing!
Mary is in me. Oh, what a wealth! Oh what a relief!
And should I not belong to her completely? Oh, what a lack of gratitude!
My beloved Savior, send me death rather than such ingratitude,
because I should die rather than live without completely belonging to Mary.
At the foot of the Cross with Saint. John the Evangelist, I accepted her a thousand times as my own mother,
and I gave her to her so many times; but if I have not done it the way you wish, beloved Jesus, I am now resuming this sacrifice in the way that You would have wanted it. And if you see anything in my soul or in my body that doesn't belong to this mighty Queen, I pray to You,
that you remove it and reject it far, because everything that is inside me and does not belong to Mary is unworthy of You.
O Holy Spirit, grant me all these favors. Plant a Tree of real life in my soul, which is Mary; cultivate and care for it so that it can grow, cover with flowers,
and bring fruit of life in abundance.
O Holy Spirit, give me a deep devotion to Mary, Your faithful Bride,
give me great trust in her motherly heart and a safe refuge in her mercy, so that thanks to her you can truly shape Jesus Christ in me,
great and mighty, until his time was completely filled. Amen.
