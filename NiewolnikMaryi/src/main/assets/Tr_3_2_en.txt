<h3> Treatise on the Perfect Devotion to the Blessed Virgin Mary, Part II, 1 </h3>
<br/>
Is it not regrettable, my beloved Master, when one sees ignorance and ignorance on earth reigning upon Your holy Mother? I am not talking about idolaters and pagans who, not knowing you, do not try to know her; nor am I talking about heretics and dissenters who, having separated themselves from you and your holy Church, do not care for the devotion to your holy Mother; but I am talking about Catholics and even scholars, who are called to teach others and do not know you or your holy Mother themselves, except in a purely rational, dry and indifferent manner. They rarely speak of your holy Mother and of the devotion we should have for her, because, as they say, they fear that it will not be abused and you will be offended, giving too much honor to your holy Mother. When they see or hear that some devotee of the Blessed Virgin often speaks of devotion to this good Mother with tenderness, strength and conviction, as a sure and free from illusion, as a short and safe path, as a path immaculate, as a miraculous mystery in order to find you and love you perfectly - they will rise up against him and give thousands of false proofs to show him that he should not talk about the Blessed Virgin so much, that there are great abuses in this respect, that efforts should be made to eliminate them.
So if so far I have not said anything in honor of Your holy Mother, "give me now the favors that I may praise her worthily," against all enemies, who are also your enemies; that I may joyfully say with the saints: "Let him not expect the mercy of God who offends His holy Mother."
67. Make me love You ardently, then through Your mercy I will obtain the grace of true devotion to Your Holy Mother and I will enkindle the whole world with it. Receive, then, the fervent prayer that I bring to you together with St. Augustine: "You are Christ, my holy Father" "Tu es Christus, Pater meus sanctus ..." (Meditationum lib.I, cap.XVIII, no.2)

<h3> "You are Christ, my holy Father" - Saint. Augustyn - translation - Helena Brownsfordowa </h3>
<br/>
<br/>
"You are Christ, my holy Father, my merciful God, my great King, my good Shepherd,
My only Master, My best Helper, Beloved my beautiful, my living Bread,
my Priest forever, my guide to the homeland, my true Light, my holy sweetness, my straight road, my wisdom shining with brightness, my undefiled simplicity, my undisturbed peace,
my whole Guard, my inheritance dear to me, my eternal salvation ...
Christ Jesus, my most beloved Lord, why have I loved and desired in all my life anything but You, my God? Where was I when my mind and heart were not with you? It will be different from now on. You, all my desires, get on fire and fly to the Lord Jesus; run, enough delay; hurry towards your goal; seek the one you are looking for.
Jesus, Cursed be those who do not love You; let those who do not love you be bitter ...
O sweet Jesus, let him love You, let him rejoice in You, let all reason admired You, devoted to Your glory. God of my heart and my part, Christ Jesus, let my own spirit die in my heart,
and you live in me; let the living charcoal of Your love burn in my womb;
let it increase and be kindled with a mighty fire; let it burn constantly on the altar of my heart,
let it glow in all my being and let it burn in the depths of my soul.
May I, on the day of my death, appear perfect before You ... Amen. "

<h3>Prayer to Jesus Christ</h3>
<b>St. Ludwik Maria Grignon de Montfort - translated by Magdalena Tuszyńska, from an English text</b>
<br/>
<br/>
O beloved Jesus, allow me to bow deeply to You, thank you for the grace You have given me, giving me Your Holy Mother through the service of Holy Slavery.
Thanks to this, she can be my lawyer before Your Majesty and support in my infinite misery. OH lord I am so devastated that without this beloved Mother I would inevitably be lost.
Yes, I need Mary by your side and all around me: that she would alleviate your righteous anger,
because I have been against you so often; that she would save me from the eternal punishment of Your righteousness which I owe rightly; so that she could ponder you, address you
to pray to you and to appease you; that she could help me save my soul and the souls of others; In a word, I need Mary, so that I can always do Your Holy Will and strive for Your greatest glory in everything.
Ah, if I could spread the mercy you have shown to me all over the world!
That everyone would know that if it wasn't for Mary, I would be condemned to perpetual damnation!
If I could offer the due thank you for such a great blessing!
Mary is in me. Oh, what a wealth! Oh what a relief!
And should I not belong to her completely? Oh, what a lack of gratitude!
My beloved Savior, send me death rather than such ingratitude,
because I should die rather than live without completely belonging to Mary.
At the foot of the Cross with Saint. John the Evangelist, I accepted her a thousand times as my own mother,
and I gave her to her so many times; but if I have not done it the way you wish, beloved Jesus, I am now resuming this sacrifice in the way that You would have wanted it. And if you see anything in my soul or in my body that doesn't belong to this mighty Queen, I pray to You,
that you remove it and reject it far, because everything that is inside me and does not belong to Mary is unworthy of You.
O Holy Spirit, grant me all these favors. Plant a Tree of real life in my soul, which is Mary; cultivate and care for it so that it can grow, cover with flowers,
and bring fruit of life in abundance.
O Holy Spirit, give me a deep devotion to Mary, Your faithful Bride,
give me great trust in her motherly heart and a safe refuge in her mercy, so that thanks to her you can truly shape Jesus Christ in me,
great and mighty, until his time was completely filled. Amen.
