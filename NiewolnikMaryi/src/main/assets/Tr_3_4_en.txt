<h3> On following Christ, Book III, Ch. 5, 1-2; 6, 1-4; 56, 1-2. </h3>
<br/>
<h3> Chap. 5, 1. </h3>
<br/>
Student. I bless you, Heavenly Father, our Lord Jesus Christ, that you have deigned to remember me as a poor man. Father of mercy and God of all consolation, (cf. 2 Cor 1: 3), thank you that you sometimes deign to strengthen me with your consolation. I bless and glorify you always with your only-begotten Son and with your Holy Spirit, the Comforter, for ever and ever.
<br/>
<br/>
2. Lord and my God! My love! When you enter my heart, everything in my heart rejoices. You are my glory, the joy of my heart, you became my guardian and my refuge in the day of my trouble. (Cf. Ps 3: 4; 118,111; 58.17). I am still weak in love and imperfect in virtue, therefore I need your strength and consolation; deign therefore to visit me more often and enlighten me with Your holy teaching. Free me from evil passions, heal my heart from disordered feelings, so that I may be internally healed and cleansed, so that I may be able to love, courageous in suffering and persistent in striving for perfection.
<br/>
<br/>
<h3> Chap. 6, 1. </h3>
<br/>
Christ. Son, you are not yet strong and discreet in love. 2. Pupil. Why Lord?
<br/>
<br/>
3. Christ. For a slight adversity, you give in to the work that has begun and you are too greedy for solace. The one who truly loves remains steadfast in the midst of temptation and does not believe the enemy's deceptive persuasions. Love me in prosperity as well as in adversity. <br> </br>
<br/>
<br/>
4. The one who loves prudently looks not so much at the gift as at the love of the giver. He cares more about his heart than about the value of the gift; above all gifts he puts the beloved. Whoever loves nobly does not find complete happiness in the gifts received, but in me above all gift. Do not think that all is lost if you do not feel love for Me or for My saints. The sweet and blissful feeling you sometimes experience is the fruit of the grace I give you and a kind of foretaste of the heavenly homeland; however, you cannot rely on it, because it suddenly comes and unexpectedly passes. On the other hand, fighting bad emotions, contempt for satanic temptations is a sign of great virtue and merit.
<br/>
<br/>
<h3> Chap. 56, 1. </h3>
<br/>
Christ. Son, if you can come out of yourself, you will be able to unite with Me. Just as giving up the lust for external things produces inner peace, so inner self-denial unites with God. I want to teach you to perfectly deny yourself and submit to My will without objection or complaint. Follow me (Mt 9: 9); I am the way and the truth and the life (Jn 14: 6). You don't walk in the wilderness,
without truth - he does not know, without life - he is dead. I am the way to go, the truth to believe, the life to be expected. I am a path that cannot be corrected, an infallible truth, a life without end. I am the simplest way, the highest truth, the true life, the blessed life, the uncreated life. If you follow My way, you will know the truth, and the truth will set you free and you will come to eternal life.
<br/>
<br/>
2. If you want to come into life, keep the commandments. (Mt 19:17). If you want to know the truth, believe Me. If you want to be perfect, go sell what you have. (Mt 19:21). If you want to be My disciple, deny yourself. (Cf. Lk 9:23). If you want a happy life, despise this life. If you want to be lifted up in heaven, humble yourself on earth. If you want to reign with Me, you must bear the cross with Me. For only the servants of the cross find the way to happiness and true light.

<h3>Prayer to Jesus Christ</h3>
<b>St. Ludwik Maria Grignon de Montfort - translated by Magdalena Tuszyńska, from an English text</b>
<br/>
<br/>
O beloved Jesus, allow me to bow deeply to You, thank you for the grace You have given me, giving me Your Holy Mother through the service of Holy Slavery.
Thanks to this, she can be my lawyer before Your Majesty and support in my infinite misery. OH lord I am so devastated that without this beloved Mother I would inevitably be lost.
Yes, I need Mary by your side and all around me: that she would alleviate your righteous anger,
because I have been against you so often; that she would save me from the eternal punishment of Your righteousness which I owe rightly; so that she could ponder you, address you
to pray to you and to appease you; that she could help me save my soul and the souls of others; In a word, I need Mary, so that I can always do Your Holy Will and strive for Your greatest glory in everything.
Ah, if I could spread the mercy you have shown to me all over the world!
That everyone would know that if it wasn't for Mary, I would be condemned to perpetual damnation!
If I could offer the due thank you for such a great blessing!
Mary is in me. Oh, what a wealth! Oh what a relief!
And should I not belong to her completely? Oh, what a lack of gratitude!
My beloved Savior, send me death rather than such ingratitude,
because I should die rather than live without completely belonging to Mary.
At the foot of the Cross with Saint. John the Evangelist, I accepted her a thousand times as my own mother,
and I gave her to her so many times; but if I have not done it the way you wish, beloved Jesus, I am now resuming this sacrifice in the way that You would have wanted it. And if you see anything in my soul or in my body that doesn't belong to this mighty Queen, I pray to You,
that you remove it and reject it far, because everything that is inside me and does not belong to Mary is unworthy of You.
O Holy Spirit, grant me all these favors. Plant a Tree of real life in my soul, which is Mary; cultivate and care for it so that it can grow, cover with flowers,
and bring fruit of life in abundance.
O Holy Spirit, give me a deep devotion to Mary, Your faithful Bride,
give me great trust in her motherly heart and a safe refuge in her mercy, so that thanks to her you can truly shape Jesus Christ in me,
great and mighty, until his time was completely filled. Amen.
