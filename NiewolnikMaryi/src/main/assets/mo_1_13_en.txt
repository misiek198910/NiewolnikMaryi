<h1> Let us praise the Mother, let us sing <h1 />
<br/>
You, who once spoke with Moses in Sinai, have now taken clean flesh in the Immaculate Virgin, free from all sin. You, who once fed on the manna of Israel, now eat the milk of poor Mother who, o miracle! - she did not recognize her husband. You who formerly scolded monarchs, now to escape from the hands of an evil king, you must flee to Egypt at night.
You, who sit on the magnificent throne, are now lying on the straw in this stable manger, hiding Your Divine Majesty from the world. And we, full of faith, praise Your Mother and sing to You, Child
